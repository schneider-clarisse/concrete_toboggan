Sponsorship

Interested in Sponsoring our Team?

Taking part in the Canadian National Concrete Canoe Competition requires extensive planning, hard work, and funding. To make our mission a success we require funding for building materials, registration fees, transportation, accommodations, and more.

This is where we would appreciate your support. Our team welcomes financial, service related, and in-kind donations. Your donation will be vital in reaching our race budget of $15,000. Sponsors of our team will gain extensive visibility throughout the University of Waterloo campus and on our race day uniforms at the competition.

Please refer to the Sponsorship Levels page to see the benefits of your donation as well as our Sponsorship Brochure for more information.

If you have any additional questions, please contact sponsorcanoe@uwaterloo.ca

Sponsorship Tiers

Thank you for your interest in sponsoring our team! Below you will find the benefits of your donation.

Concrete ($2000+)

All the benefits of Platinum, plus:

  • Promotional Opportunity: talk with us about what we can do to promote you
  • Canoe and team members available for company promotional events (schedule permitting)
  • Access to team resumes for recruitment purposes

Platinum ($1500 - $1999)

All the benefits of Gold, plus:

  • A copy of our team t-shirt sent to you
  • Company Swag handed out to competitors

Gold ($1000 - $1499)

All the benefits of Silver, plus:

  • Company logo on our t-shirts and race-day uniforms
  • Company logo at weekly campus events
  • Thank-you sponsorship plaque for display

Silver ($250 - $999)

All the benefits of Bronze, plus:

  • Company logo on our website (linked to your website)
  • Company name on our t-shirts and race-day uniforms
  • Company name at weekly campus events

Bronze (Under $250)

  • Company name on our website (linked to your website)
  • Exclusive invitation to race day events
  • Thank you sponsorship certificate
  • Team updates every quarter
